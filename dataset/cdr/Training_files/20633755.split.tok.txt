Suxamethonium induced prolonged apnea in a patient receiving electroconvulsive therapy .
Suxamethonium causes prolonged apnea in patients in whom pseudocholinesterase enzyme gets deactivated by organophosphorus ( OP ) poisons .
Here , we present a similar incident in a severely depressed patient who received electroconvulsive therapy ( ECT ) .
Prolonged apnea in our case ensued because the information about suicidal attempt by OP compound was concealed from the treating team .
