Twin preterm neonates with cardiac toxicity related to lopinavir / ritonavir therapy .
We report twin neonates who were born prematurely at 32 weeks of gestation to a mother with human immunodeficiency virus infection .
One of the twins developed complete heart block and dilated cardiomyopathy related to lopinavir / ritonavir therapy , a boosted protease - inhibitor agent , while the other twin developed mild bradycardia .
We recommend caution in the use of lopinavir / ritonavir in the immediate neonatal period .
