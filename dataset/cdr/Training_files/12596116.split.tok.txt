Octreotide - induced hypoxemia and pulmonary hypertension in premature neonates .
The authors report 2 cases of premature neonates who had enterocutaneous fistula complicating necrotizing enterocolitis .
Pulmonary hypertension developed after administration of a somatostatin analogue , octreotide , to enhance resolution of the fistula .
The authors discuss the mechanism of the occurrence of this complication and recommend caution of its use in high - risk premature neonates .
