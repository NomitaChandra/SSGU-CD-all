The site of common side effects of sumatriptan .
Atypical sensations following the use of subcutaneous sumatriptan are common , but of uncertain origin .
They are almost always benign , but can be mistaken for a serious adverse event by the patient .
Two patients are presented with tingling or burning sensations limited to areas of heat exposure or sunburn .
In these individuals , side effects are most likely generated superficially in the skin .
