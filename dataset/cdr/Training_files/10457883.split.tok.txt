Recurarization in the recovery room .
A case of recurarization in the recovery room is reported .
Accumulation of atracurium in the intravenous line led to recurarization after flushing the line in the recovery room .
A respiratory arrest with severe desaturation and bradycardia occurred .
Circumstances leading to this event and the mechanisms enabling a neuromuscular blockade to occur , following the administration of a small dose of relaxant , are discussed .
