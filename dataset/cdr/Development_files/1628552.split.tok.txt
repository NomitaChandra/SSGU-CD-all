Chloroquine related complete heart block with blindness : case report .
A 27 - year old African woman with history of regular chloroquine ingestion presented with progressive deterioration of vision , easy fatiguability , dyspnoea , dizziness progressing to syncopal attacks .
Ophthalmological assessment revealed features of chloroquine retinopathy , cardiac assessment revealed features of heart failure and a complete heart block with right bundle branch block pattern .
The heart block was treated by pacemaker insertion and the heart failure resolved spontaneously following chloroquine discontinuation .
She however remains blind .
