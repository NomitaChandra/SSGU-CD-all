Seizure after flumazenil administration in a pediatric patient .
Flumazenil is a benzodiazepine receptor antagonist used to reverse sedation and respiratory depression induced by benzodiazepines .
Seizures and cardiac arrhythmias have complicated its use in adult patients .
Overdose patients who have coingested tricyclic antidepressants have a higher risk of these complications .
Little information exists concerning adverse effects of flumazenil in children .
We report the occurrence of a generalized tonic - clonic seizure in a pediatric patient following the administration of flumazenil .
