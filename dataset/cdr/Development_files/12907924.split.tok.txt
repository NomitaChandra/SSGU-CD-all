Methylphenidate - induced obsessive - compulsive symptoms in an elderly man .
An 82 - year - old man with treatment - resistant depression and early Alzheimer 's disease was started on methylphenidate .
Significant obsessive - compulsive behavior ensued but diminished over several weeks when methylphenidate was replaced by fluvoxamine .
The patient had no prior psychiatric history , but he had a sister with obsessive - compulsive disorder .
It appears that methylphenidate precipitated the patient 's pathological behavior .
