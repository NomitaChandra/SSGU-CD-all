Quinidine phenylethylbarbiturate - induced fulminant hepatitis in a pregnant woman .
A case report .
We report the case of a 19 - year - old Laotian patient affected by fulminant hepatitis during the third trimester of her pregnancy after a 1 - month administration of quinidine phenylethylbarbiturate .
After delivery , the patient underwent orthotopic liver transplantation .
The patient was in good condition 16 months after liver transplantation .
Quinidine itself or phenylethylbarbiturate may be responsible for fulminant hepatitis in this patient .
