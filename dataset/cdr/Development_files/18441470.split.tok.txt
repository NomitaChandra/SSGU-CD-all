Complete atrioventricular block secondary to lithium therapy .
Sinus node dysfunction has been reported most frequently among the adverse cardiovascular effects of lithium .
In the present case , complete atrioventricular ( AV ) block with syncopal attacks developed secondary to lithium therapy , necessitating permanent pacemaker implantation .
Serum lithium levels remained under or within the therapeutic range during the syncopal attacks .
Lithium should be used with extreme caution , especially in patients with mild disturbance of AV conduction .
