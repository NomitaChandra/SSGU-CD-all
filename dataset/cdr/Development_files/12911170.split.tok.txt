Ciprofloxacin - induced acute interstitial nephritis and autoimmune hemolytic anemia .
Ciprofloxacin has been associated with several side effects including interstitial nephritis and hemolytic anemia .
The combination of both side effects is extremely rare .
In this report , we describe a case of ciprofloxacin - induced interstitial nephritis and autoimmune hemolytic anemia .
Hemolytic anemia improved after stopping the drug and initiation of steroid therapy .
Unfortunately , acute interstitial nephritis was irreversible and the patient developed end - stage renal disease .
