Phenytoin induced fatal hepatic injury .
A 61 year old female developed fatal hepatic failure after phenytoin administration .
A typical multisystem clinical pattern precedes the manifestations of hepatic injury .
The hematologic , biochemical and pathologic features indicate a mixed hepatocellular damage due to drug hypersensitivity .
In a patient receiving phenytoin who presents a viral - like illness , early recognition and discontinuation of the drug are mandatory .
